MandiGo (draft) Data Policy

Last Updated: {% now "F Y" %}

1. Purpose of Data Collection

MandiGo collects, stores, and displays price submissions from users to improve market transparency. The goal is to help farmers, consumers, and traders view real-time prices for vegetables and crops across various locations. Data is used for public display, analytics, and service improvement.

2. Types of Data Collected

a) User-Submitted Price Data
  • Commodity or vegetable name
  • Quantity and unit
  • Price entered by the user
  • Location
  • Date and time of submission
  • Image of the crop (optional)
  • Optional remarks or quality details
b) User Information
  • Username or alias
  • Email or mobile number (for registration and verification)
  • Device or technical information (IP address, timestamp, browser type)

3. How the Data is Used

  • Display aggregated price data publicly to all users.
  • Use submissions for analytics, trend tracking, and reporting.
  • Detect and prevent fake or duplicate entries.
  • Communicate updates, notifications, or insights to registered users.
  • Share anonymised and aggregated data with partners for research or policy purposes.

4. Data Display and Sharing

  • Price data entered by users is visible to everyone on the platform, with anonymity maintained.
  • User names, mobile numbers, or emails are not publicly shown.
  • Aggregated or averaged data (e.g., “Average tomato price in Nagpur”) may be displayed publicly.
  • No personal information will be sold or shared with third parties.
  • Data shared with partners will be anonymised and used only for legitimate research or improvement purposes.

5. User Responsibilities

  • Users must submit genuine and accurate data.
  • Users must not post false or misleading information to manipulate market prices.
  • Users must not share personal information of others without their consent.
  • Repeated misuse or false submissions may lead to account suspension.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Data will be retained as long as required for service operation and analysis.
  • Users may request deletion of their personal information by emailing the support team.
  • Aggregated and anonymised data may continue to be used for analytics and reporting even after account deletion.

7. Privacy and Security

  • Personal data is stored securely with encryption and restricted access.
  • MandiGo uses security measures to prevent unauthorised access, loss, or misuse of data.
  • In the event of a data breach, affected users will be notified in accordance with applicable laws.

8. Consent

By using the MandiGo platform and submitting data, users consent to the collection, use, and display of information as described in this policy. Users can withdraw consent by deactivating their account.

9. Third-Party Data Usage

MandiGo may use third-party tools (e.g., analytics, map services) that process limited technical data. All partners comply with applicable data protection standards.

Aggregated and anonymised data may be shared with government bodies or research organisations to promote transparency.

10. Changes to This Policy

This Data Policy may be updated periodically. Major updates will be communicated via the platform or email. Continued use of MandiGo after updates indicates acceptance of the revised terms and conditions.

11. Contact Us
